How Young4ChickS Works

1. Register as a Young Farmer

Create your profile with your NIN and recommender details to qualify.

2. Request Your Chicks

Choose between broilers or layers, and submit your request online.
3. Track Your Request

Monitor approval status and get notified when chicks are ready for pickup.

Who Can Join Young4ChickS?

To participate in the program, youth must meet the following criteria:

Aged 18–30

You must be a youth living in the constituency within the eligible age bracket.

Registered with a Recommender

Provide your recommender’s name and NIN number for accountability.

Submit a Chick Request Form

Only registered farmers can request chicks — once every 4 months.
