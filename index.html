About Us

Young4ChickS is a youth-driven urban poultry initiative that empowers young people aged 20–30 through accessible and sustainable agribusiness. Founded by a passionate Youth Member of Parliament, our goal is to support youth in urban areas to kick-start wealth creation through modern chick rearing.

We specialize in providing affordable broiler and layer chicks, both local and exotic, along with access to feeds and continuous support. Every starter farmer is eligible for 100 chicks, with returning farmers receiving up to 500 chicks, creating an inclusive and scalable model for growth.

At Young4ChickS, we believe in creating opportunity, building self-reliance, and transforming lives one chick at a time. Through our reliable brooding system, dedicated support staff, and transparent operations, we are laying the foundation for a youth-led money economy that thrives in every urban community.
